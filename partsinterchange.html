Sony Mavica Interchangeable Parts Guide
(Excluding Batteries)

Mavica Cameras Only

General Notes

  • Mechanical and electronic compatibility is usually best within the same generation and model family.
  • Parts like floppy drives, LCD screens, lens assemblies, and buttons are sometimes swappable within a series, but model-specific plastics and firmware may limit full compatibility.
  • CD and FD series are not compatible with each other for storage drives, boards, or shells.


FD5/FD7/FD51 Series (1997)

Part Interchangeable? Notes
Floppy Drive Yes (FD5/FD7/FD51) Uses standard 1.44MB mechanism
LCD Screen Yes Same size and connector
Button/Keypads Yes Same physical layout
Main Board No (part-numbered) Firmware/model-specific
Lens Assembly No Different specs
Shell/Housing No Cosmetic/model labeling differs

FD71/FD73/FD81/FD83/FD85/FD75 Family (1998–1999)

Part Interchangeable? Notes
Floppy Drive Yes (all models) Standard Sony drive, easy swap
LCD Screen Yes (most models) FD71/73/81/83/85/75 share screens
Button/Keypads Yes Same design
Lens Assembly Partial FD81/83/85 (3x/6x) sometimes; check specs
Main Board No Model-specific
Shell Parts Partial (81/83/85) Cosmetic differences, fit may vary
Flash Assembly Yes (most models) Same design

FD87/FD88/FD90/FD92/FD95/FD97 Family (1999–2000)

Part Interchangeable? Notes
Floppy Drive Yes (all models) Physically compatible
LCD Screen Yes (all models) Same form factor and ribbon
Button/Keypads Yes Same physical layout
Lens Assembly Partial FD95/97/92 (8x/10x), FD87/88/90 (6x) may swap among close siblings
Main Board No Different chipsets/firmware
Memory Stick Slot Yes (hybrid models) FD92/95/97
Shell/Housing Partial (FD95/97/92) Cosmetic/model-specific, but close fit
Flash Assembly Yes (all models)

FD100/FD200/FD300 (2001–2002, Hybrid Models)

Part Interchangeable? Notes
Floppy Drive Yes As before
LCD Screen Yes Same size
Memory Stick Slot Yes
Button/Keypads Yes
Main Board No
Shell/Housing No

CD Series (CD1000, CD200, CD250, CD350, CD400, CD500)

Part Interchangeable? Notes
CD Drive Partial CD200/250/350 similar; CD1000 is unique
LCD Screen Yes (200/250/350) Same screen, ribbon, housing
Button/Keypads Yes (200/250/350)
Lens Assembly No Zeiss lenses (CD400/500) are exclusive
Main Board No Firmware/model-specific
Flash Assembly Partial CD400/500 share; others differ
Shell/Housing No Model-specific

MVC-300, Prototypes, SLR

Part Interchangeable? Notes
Most Parts No Unique, rare, collector-only components
Floppy/CD Drive No Proprietary/early design

Collector Tips

  • Floppy Drives and LCDs are the most likely to be compatible within a generation.
  • Lens blocks and main boards are the least likely to swap successfully.
  • Always check part numbers and ribbon connector orientation before swapping.
  • Cosmetic parts (doors, covers) often have slight differences between models, even within a series.

Sony Mavica Interchangeable Parts Guide (Excluding Batteries)

General Notes

  • Mechanical and electronic compatibility is usually best within the same generation and model family.
  • Parts like floppy drives, LCD screens, lens assemblies, and buttons are sometimes swappable within a series, but main boards and plastics often differ.
  • CD and FD series are not compatible with each other for storage drives, boards, or shells.
Part Interchangeable? Notes
Floppy Drive Yes (FD5/FD7/FD51) Uses standard 1.44MB mechanism
LCD Screen Yes Same size and connector
Main Board No Firmware/model-specific

Collector Tips

  • Floppy drives and LCDs are the most likely to be compatible within a generation.
  • Lens blocks and main boards are the least likely to swap successfully.
  • Always check part numbers and ribbon connector orientation before swapping.

Legend: Yes = Interchangeable within series, P = Partial (check sub-model), No = Not interchangeable

Parts: what to expect

Parts for sale and some aftermarket replacements for Sony Mavica cameras, though availability depends heavily on the model, part, and the current market. Here’s a breakdown of what you can expect:

  • Batteries: Aftermarket NP-F330/F550 and NP-FM50 InfoLithium batteries are widely available.
  • Battery Chargers: Aftermarket chargers and USB adapters are common.
  • Floppy Drives: Replacement 1.44MB floppy drives can be found; PC drives can sometimes be adapted.
  • Belts & Screws: Generic belts and screws often fit.
  • LCD Screens & Lens Assemblies: Usually only via salvaged donor cameras.

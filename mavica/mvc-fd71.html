Sony Mavica MVC-FD71

About the FD71:
The Sony MVC-FD71 is part of the classic Mavica line that used 3.5" floppy disks for digital photo storage. Many features are shared with other Mavica models of this era, but the FD71 is distinguished by its lens, LCD screen size, and other details as listed below. All specs and features are taken directly from the official Sony MVC-FD71 manual.

Recording medium: 3.5" floppy disk (2HD, 1.44 MB)
Picture element: 640 × 480 pixels
Image file type: JPEG
Lens: Sony lens, f=5.5 – 55 mm, F2.8 – 2.9
LCD monitor: 2.5-inch, TFT color
Viewfinder: Real-image type
Battery: Sony InfoLithium battery pack NP-F330
Dimensions: Approx. 154 × 103 × 157 mm (W × H × D)
Mass: Approx. 650 g (camera only)
Other features:
  • Self-timer function
  • Date/time recording
  • Automatic white balance
  • Built-in flash
  • Video output (NTSC/PAL selectable)
  • Serial interface (RS-232C for PC connection)

Sony Official Manual (PDF)

Note:
Compared to other models in the same series, the FD71 features a lens with a focal range of f=5.5–55 mm and a 2.5-inch LCD monitor. All other major specifications, including the floppy disk recording system, remain the same.

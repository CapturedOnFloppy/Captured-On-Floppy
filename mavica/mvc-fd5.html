Picture element: 640 × 480 pixels
Image file type: JPEG
Lens: Sony lens, f=4.2 – 42 mm, F2.8 – 2.9
LCD monitor: 2.5-inch, TFT color
Viewfinder: Real-image type
Battery: Sony InfoLithium battery pack NP-F330
Dimensions: Approx. 154 × 103 × 157 mm (W × H × D)
Mass: Approx. 650 g (camera only)
Other features:
  • Self-timer function
  • Date/time recording
  • Automatic white balance
  • Built-in flash
  • Video output (NTSC/PAL selectable)
  • Serial interface (RS-232C for PC connection)

Sony Official Manual (PDF)

Difference between FD5 and FD7:
The MVC-FD5 uses a traditional optical viewfinder, while the MVC-FD7 features a color LCD electronic viewfinder. All other major specifications are nearly identical.

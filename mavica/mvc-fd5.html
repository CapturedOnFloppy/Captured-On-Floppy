Sony MVC-FD5

About the Camera

The Sony MVC-FD5 was a landmark digital camera, notable for its use of 3.5" floppy disks as storage and its role in the evolution of consumer digital photography.

Key Features

  • Year Released: 1997
  • Image Sensor: 1/4-inch CCD, 0.3MP
  • Lens: 47mm equivalent, fixed focus
  • Storage: 3.5" Floppy Disk
  • Image Format: JPEG
  • Power: Sony Infolithium battery
  • Notes: First consumer Mavica floppy camera

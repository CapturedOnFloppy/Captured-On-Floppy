Sony Mavica MVC-FD95

About the FD95:
The Sony MVC-FD95 is a professional-grade member of the Mavica line, using 3.5" floppy disks for digital photo storage. The FD95 is notable for its high-resolution imaging, powerful zoom lens, and advanced manual controls for creative photography. All specifications and features are taken from the official Sony MVC-FD95 manual.

Recording medium: 3.5" floppy disk (2HD, 1.44 MB)
Picture element: 1600 × 1200 pixels (High), 1024 × 768 pixels (Standard), 640 × 480 pixels (Economy)
Image file type: JPEG
Lens: Sony lens, f=8.0 – 64 mm, F2.8 – 3.0 (8x optical zoom)
LCD monitor: 2.5-inch, TFT color
Viewfinder: Real-image type
Battery: Sony InfoLithium battery pack NP-F550/NP-F330
Dimensions: Approx. 162 × 112 × 160 mm (W × H × D)
Mass: Approx. 880 g (camera only)
Other features:
  • Self-timer function
  • Date/time recording
  • Automatic white balance
  • Built-in flash
  • Video output (NTSC/PAL selectable)
  • Serial interface (RS-232C for PC connection)
  • Program AE (auto exposure)
  • Red-eye reduction
  • Spot metering
  • Macro mode
  • 8x optical zoom
  • Continuous shooting mode
  • Quick review
  • Auto power off
  • High-resolution mode (1600 × 1200 pixels)
  • Standard-resolution mode (1024 × 768 pixels)
  • Economy-resolution mode (640 × 480 pixels)
  • Manual focus
  • Manual exposure
  • AE lock
  • Custom white balance

Sony Official Manual (PDF)

Note:
Compared to previous models, the FD95 offers even higher resolution, 8x optical zoom lens, and extensive manual controls. The camera supports multiple image sizes for flexible shooting. All other major specifications, including the floppy disk recording system, remain the same.
